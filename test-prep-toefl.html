🧾Introduction to the TOEFL
The Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) evaluates non-native English speakers on their ability to read, listen, speak, and write in English. Universities across the US, Canada, and beyond rely on TOEFL scores for admissions.

GoFar’s TOEFL prep program is designed to help you understand test structures, practice under exam conditions, and achieve top scores.

Is GoFar's TOEFL program suitable for beginners?

Yes! Whether you’re taking TOEFL for the first time or looking to improve your score, we’ll assess your level and create a personalized prep path. 

Does the course prepare for the new shorter TOEFL iBT format?

Absolutely. We use updated materials that reflect the current test format, ensuring your prep matches what you'll see on exam day.

Yes. Our instructors provide detailed written and audio feedback on your speaking and writing responses.
