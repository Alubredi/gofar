💬 IELTS Prep – FAQ

What is the difference between IELTS Academic and General Training?

IELTS Academic is for those applying for higher education or professional registration, while General Training is for migration or work purposes. GoFar prepares students for both formats. 

How long is the IELTS course with GoFar?

Our standard IELTS program is 4 weeks, but it can be extended or fast-tracked depending on your timeline and goals.

Will I get practice with speaking and writing?

Yes! You’ll receive personalized feedback on Speaking and Writing tasks weekly, with mock tests, video reviews, and one-on-one coaching. 

Can GoFar guarantee a Band 7 or 8?

While no institution can guarantee a specific score, our structured approach and experienced trainers have helped many students consistently reach Band 7.5+.
