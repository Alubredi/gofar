Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services across our websites, applications, and platforms.

By using GoFar’s services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please read it carefully 

1. Information We Collect

GoFar collects the minimum amount of information necessary to provide you with the services you request. This may include, but is not limited to:
🎯 Personal Information: Name, contact details (email, phone number), mailing address, and user account details.
🎯 Payment Information: If you make a purchase, we may collect payment details such as debit/credit card information and billing address. Payment processing is handled by third-party providers, and GoFar does not directly store or manage these details.
🎯 Other Information: We may collect additional data such as your IP address, browser type, and usage data through cookies (described below). 

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:
🎯 Provide Services: To process your requests, payments, and service registrations (e.g., exams, training, etc.).
🎯 Improve User Experience: To enhance and personalize your experience on our websites and apps.
🎯 Communication: To send you service-related notifications, updates, and promotional offers (if you opt-in).
🎯 Security and Support: To ensure the security of our platforms and provide customer support. 

3. Information Security

GoFar takes the security and privacy of your information seriously. We implement various security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure server technologies, and other industry-standard practices.
We may ask for additional forms of identity verification if you wish to access or update your account information, ensuring the integrity and security of your data. 

4. Cookies

GoFar uses cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small files that store information on your device and allow us to recognize you during future visits.

There are different types of cookies used on our websites:
🎯 First-Party Cookies: Set by GoFar's websites to track your activities and preferences.
🎯 Third-Party Cookies: Set by other websites or platforms (e.g., YouTube, payment gateways) embedded on our site. GoFar does not control these cookies, and you should review the privacy policies of these third parties for more information.
🎯 Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted after you close your browser.
🎯 Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a specified period, unless deleted manually. 

5. Disabling Cookies

You have the option to manage cookies via your browser settings. If you choose to block cookies, some website functionalities may not be available. Here are links to cookie management resources for major browsers:

🎯 Firefox

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website. 

6. Sharing Your Information

GoFar does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:
🎯 Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers to process payments, perform customer support, or help with marketing.
🎯 Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or in response to valid legal processes.
🎯 Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of GoFar, your personal data may be transferred to the new entity.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. GoFar is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

8. Access and Control Over Your Data

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to update your account information or exercise your rights, please contact us at gofaroperations@gmail.com.

9. Children’s Privacy

GoFar does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately so that we can take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

GoFar reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make changes, we will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Effective Date." Please review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
