🌟 What is GoFar #DataKids?
GoFar #DataKids is a child-friendly learning hub designed to introduce foundational STEM skills in a creative, age-appropriate, and engaging way.

The program provides:

🎯 Project-based learning in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Engineering & Tech
🎯 Age-graded modules for beginner, intermediate, and advanced learners
🎯 A balance of fun learning, teamwork, and critical thinking
🎯 Mentorship from top educators and STEM professionals
🎯 Safe digital environments for learning and collaboration

🧠 What Kids Will Learn

Each module is designed to teach real-world concepts while having fun.

  • 📊 Junior Data Explorers (Ages 7–9)
  • 🤖 STEM Innovators (Ages 10–12)
  • 💻 Future Tech Creators (Ages 13–14)

🎯 Introduction to Numbers & Patterns
🎯 Basic Coding with Scratch or Blockly
🎯 Storytelling with Data (charts, colors, and visuals)
🎯 Problem-solving games and brain boosters 

🎯 Introduction to Robotics & AI Concepts
🎯 Coding in Python for Beginners
🎯 Basic Data Analysis (graphs, trends, predictions)
🎯 Building mini projects (e.g., Smart Weather Station, Code-A-Bot) 

🎯 Intermediate Python & App Development
🎯 Data Collection & Visualization
🎯 Understanding Machine Learning & Ethics
🎯 Building and Presenting a Capstone STEM Project

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need prior tech experience?

No. The program is designed to introduce concepts from scratch, with fun learning for beginners.

Can I enroll multiple children?

Yes, and we offer multi-child discounts for families.

Is this just for kids who want to be engineers?

Not at all! STEM builds logic, creativity, and confidence for any future path.

Will my child get a certificate?

Yes, certificates and project reports are provided at the end of each completed module.
