The GRE General Test is a key requirement for many graduate programs. Our GRE prep course is designed to improve your scores through rigorous quantitative reasoning, verbal reasoning, and analytical writing practice.

GRE Curriculum Overview
🎯 Verbal Reasoning: Reading comprehension, text completion, sentence equivalence
🎯 Quantitative Reasoning: Arithmetic, algebra, geometry, data analysis
🎯 Analytical Writing: Argument and issue essays with grading rubrics.

💬 GRE Prep – FAQ

Who needs to take the GRE?

The GRE is required for admission into most U.S. and international graduate programs, especially in sciences, humanities, and social sciences. 

Does your program cover the new shorter GRE format?

Yes. We use updated prep materials to reflect the revised GRE structure introduced in late 2023. 

Is the Quant section hard? What if I’m not good at math?

Our math instructors specialize in helping non-math students master core GRE Quant concepts from scratch. You'll get all the practice and support you need. 

Will I receive individual essay feedback?

Yes. You'll submit practice essays and receive professional feedback aligned with ETS scoring guidelines.
