💼Admin Manager
We're on a mission to empower learners and professionals through expert-led test prep, training, and growth-focused services. We’re looking for a smart, efficient, and highly organized Admin Officer to join our growing team.

As Admin Officer, you’ll be the backbone of our day-to-day operations. From handling administrative tasks and supporting coordination across departments to keeping digital systems in order, you’ll help ensure things run smoothly.

🛠️ Key Responsibilities

  1. Oversee daily administrative operations and ensure smooth internal workflows
  2. Manage emails, scheduling, and communication across departments
  3. Support training and exam registration processes (data entry, payment follow-up, user support)
  4. Keep digital records and documentation organized and up to date
  5. Assist in onboarding of new staff, trainers, and students
  6. Coordinate logistics for live sessions, webinars, and trainings
  7. Monitor student or client progress reports and escalate issues where needed
  8. Support marketing and outreach efforts as needed (emails, WhatsApp, Telegram group communication, etc.)

✅ Requirements

🎯 Minimum of OND or equivalent; a Bachelor's degree is preferred

🎯 1–2 years experience in a similar administrative or support role

🎯 Strong English proficiency (written and spoken)

🎯 Reliable internet access and personal laptop for remote work

🎯 Ability to work independently with minimal supervision 

🎯 Ideal Candidate

🎯 A proactive problem-solver with strong attention to detail

🎯 Able to multitask and manage time effectively in a fast-paced environment

🎯 Excellent written and verbal communication skills

🎯 Tech-savvy with confidence using Google Workspace, Excel, forms, and basic project management tools

🎯 Passionate about education, growth, and meaningful service

🎯 Flexible and willing to learn, adapt, and take initiative

🎯 Prior experience in admin, operations, or virtual assistant roles is a plus 

🎁 What You’ll Get

🎯 A dynamic, impact-driven work environment

🎯 Flexible working hours with remote options

🎯 Opportunity for career growth and leadership development

🎯 In-house training and mentoring

🎯 A chance to help shape the future of education and digital learning in Africa and beyond
