FAQs

We’ve gathered the most common questions about our services to help guide you. If you don’t find the answer you’re looking for, feel free to reach out directly to our team – we’re here to help!
How much will studying abroad cost?

The cost of studying abroad varies depending on factors like destination, chosen course, and lifestyle. Some countries and courses have higher fees than others. We understand budgeting for studying abroad can be tricky, so our expert counselors will break down all the costs involved and help you plan a budget that works for you.

Can I apply for scholarships?

Absolutely! There are numerous scholarships available, but securing one can be competitive. We specialize in helping students find scholarship opportunities that match their profile, and our experienced counselors will guide you through the application process to improve your chances of success.

Why do I need a study abroad counselor?

Studying abroad can feel overwhelming, especially when you're unfamiliar with the process. Our counselors are experts who will guide you every step of the way—from choosing the right university and program to navigating visa processes and preparing for interviews. Their personalized guidance will ensure you’re well-prepared for your overseas adventure. 

Can I pay my tuition in installments?

Many universities offer payment plans for tuition, allowing you to spread out the payments over time. We’ll help you explore institutions that provide flexible payment options to fit your financial situation.

Can I change my course or university once I've been accepted?

While it is possible to switch courses in some instances, changing universities after acceptance can be a more complex process.
